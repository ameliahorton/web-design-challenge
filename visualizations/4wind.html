Effects of City Latitude on Weather

Latitude vs Wind Speed



Visually comparing latitude to wind speed shows somewhat of a trend. While cities with wind speeds between 0 and 25 mph exist at all latitudes, cities with higher wind speeds only exist at latitudes above 40 in this dataset. This means that a city with a latitude of 40+ has the possibility of very high wind speeds, whereas a city of a lower latitude is most likely to have wind speeds of 25 mph or less.
